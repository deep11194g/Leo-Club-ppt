The epitome of Leadership, Experience & Opportunity,

Objective



  • To provide the youth of the college an opportunity to contribute to the development of the local, national, international community.
  • Inculcate leadership qualities, communication skills, team spirit and a sense of social responsibility among members of club.
  • Come up with new and innovative ideas which can involve greater participation from students.
